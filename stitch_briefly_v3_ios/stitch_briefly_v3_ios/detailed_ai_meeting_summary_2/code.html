Q3 Performance Review

Overall performance was strong, exceeding revenue targets by 8%. However, user acquisition costs rose significantly in August, prompting a review of our current marketing channels.

Budget Reallocation

Due to the high ROI seen on recent social campaigns, the team agreed to shift funds away from traditional print media. This will provide more resources for targeted LinkedIn and Instagram ads.

Hiring Freeze

To maintain current margins ahead of the Q4 push, a temporary freeze on junior design hires was implemented. Current team members will absorb the workload, with a review scheduled for January.
